Pepperoni pizza

Homeade pizza

No matter who you are, you love pizza. It's the best.

Read on and find out how to make delicous pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Jalepenos

Steps

  1. Roll out dough
  2. Add sauce, pepperoni, jalepenos, and cheese
  3. Bake at 500 for 12 minutes
  4. Slice it up then devour it
