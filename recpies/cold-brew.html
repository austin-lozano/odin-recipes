Cold brew

Hot coffee is for your grandmother.

Read on and find out how to make refreshing, youthful cold brew.

Ingredients

  • Coffee grounds
  • Filtered water
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Oatmilk

Steps

  1. Put coffee grounds in a steeping basket, and add in a sprinkle of cinnamon and nutmeg
  2. Pour filtered water over grounds and let steep for 18 hours
  3. Remove grounds and stir cold brew
  4. Pour over ice and add a splash of oatmilk
