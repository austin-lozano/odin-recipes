Birra tacos

There's nothing quite like birra tacos. They're so simple, yet so life changing.

Read on and find out how to make them.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Consume
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Prepare consume in slow cooker
  2. Place meat in slow cooker and let cook
  3. Put finished meat on tortilla, sprinke cheese, and fry them up
  4. Dish up and enjoy those tacos
